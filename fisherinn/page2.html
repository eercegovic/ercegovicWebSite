The History...

Constructed in 1847, Fisher Inn & Suites was the original home of sawmill owner William R. Fisher.  In 1908, the Inn was sold to Dr. Glenn who used the property as a residence and Doctor's office for 45 years.

In 1989, in an effort to restore historic sprit of the nineteenth century, Fred and Brenda Ruth renovated the building into the charming Fisher Inn & Suites.

Your elegantly furnished suite includes a remote control color T.V. with cable, telephone, alarm clock, plush carpeting, completely equipped kitchen with microwave, dishwasher, coffeemaker, service for four, and of course fluffy towels and smooth linens. 

Fisher Inn and Suites also has lounges, porches, patios and decks for relaxation and enjoyment!
