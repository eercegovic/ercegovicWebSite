Artimino Estate

We have chosen Artimino, one of the Medici estates located in the heart of Tuscany, for our wedding and reception.  The estate includes the church, hotel, villa, several apartments, two swimming pools, tennis courts, walking/biking trails, restaurants, shops, a working farm, vineyard and olive grove. 

  

Rehearsal/Welcome Dinner

There will be a Rehearsal/Welcome dinner Wednesday evening at 8:00pm at the Cantina del Redi, which is part of the Artimino Estate. We hope you will join us!

Wedding

Our Wedding will take place at Pieve dei S.S. Maria e Leonardo in Artimino at Twelve Thirty in the afternoon on June 1, 2006.  The church is within walking distance from the Hotel, Apartments and Reception.
