Travel Information

Flights

Florence airport is the closest to Artimino, however it closed for renovation from February through May.  All flights during this time will be diverted to either Pisa or Bologna.   These alternate airports are also close - Pisa is 33 miles and Bologna is 75 Miles.  We suggest confirming your reservation to determine which airport you will be arriving into prior to your departure.

 Traveling from the Airport  to the Hotel:

Hire Car:

Our wedding coordinator can arrange a car to pick you up at the airport and drive you to the hotel.  Please let us know when you plan to arrive and we can help coordinate transfers for you.  Or, you can contact our coordinator directly at:

Debora Vedovi,  Regency San Marino        deborav@omniway.sm

By Rental Car:

You can also rent a car.  Most major rental companies are in Italy and cars can be picked up at the airport.  Please note that most cars have manual transmission, unless you request an automatic.
